Intraperitoneal Injection

IP injection is commonly used in animal experiments to deliver drugs or other substances to study their effects on various biological processes. This method is often preferred for drugs that have poor oral bioavailability, as it allows for direct delivery of the drug into the bloodstream. .

Cell culture

Cell culture is used extensively in biological and medical research to study the behavior of cells, to develop new drugs, and to produce biological products, such as vaccines, monoclonal antibodies, and enzymes. It is also used in regenerative medicine and tissue engineering to create functional tissues and organs in vitro.

Total RNA extraction

The extracted RNA can be used for a variety of applications, such as gene expression analysis, sequencing, and functional studies. The quality of the RNA is crucial for downstream applications, and the extracted RNA should be free from contaminants, such as genomic DNA, proteins, and chemicals that could interfere with downstream analyses.

Solid-phase peptide synthesis

SPPS is a powerful tool for the synthesis of peptides and small proteins, with applications in biochemistry, pharmacology, and drug discovery. The technique allows for the production of large quantities of peptides with high purity and enables the synthesis of modified peptides with non-natural amino acids and other modifications.
